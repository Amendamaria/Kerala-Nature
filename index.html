Shangumukam Beach

Shangumukham Beach, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is a serene coastal retreat offering a tranquil escape. Facing the Arabian Sea, the beach is renowned for its golden sands and scenic sunsets. The iconic 35-meter-long mermaid statue adds a touch of artistry to the shoreline. Visitors can indulge in leisurely strolls along the promenade, enjoying the gentle sea... breeze and picturesque views.The beach also hosts cultural events, making it a vibrant hub where nature, art, and relaxation converge to create a memorable coastal experience.

Kuttanadu

Kuttanadu, often referred to as the "Rice Bowl of Kerala," is a unique region characterized by its vast network of interconnected rivers, lakes, and canals. Situated in Alappuzha district, it is known for extensive paddy fields below sea level. The scenic landscape, marked by traditional houseboats and vibrant birdlife, offers a distinctive backwater experience. ...Kuttanadu showcases Kerala's agrarian essence, with its lush greenery and waterways, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking a tranquil and culturally rich Kerala experience.

Athirapally Waterfalls

Athirapally Waterfalls, nestled in Kerala's Western Ghats, captivates with its 24-meter plunge, forming a 100-foot-wide cascade. Surrounded by lush tropical greenery, the falls offer a tranquil retreat. The monsoon season enhances its allure, attracting visitors to witness the intensified flow.Accessible pathways lead to viewpoints, providing a mesmerizing encounter with nature's grandeur.... A visit to Athirapally promises a brief escape into the enchanting beauty of Kerala's landscapes, making it a cherished destination for nature lovers and seekers of serene experiences.

Munnar

Munnar, a serene hill station in Kerala, India, captivates visitors with its lush tea plantations, mist-covered hills, and cool climate. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it offers a picturesque escape for nature enthusiasts. The vast tea estates create a vibrant green tapestry, complemented by attractions like the Mattupetty Dam and Anamudi Peak. Beyond the scenic landscapes,... Munnar reveals its cultural charm, providing a delightful blend of tranquility and local experiences for those seeking respite in the lap of South India's captivating beauty.

Periyar

Periyar, situated in Kerala's Western Ghats, is a haven for nature enthusiasts. Home to the famous Periyar National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary, this lush region offers a rich biodiversity with diverse flora and fauna. The centerpiece is the Periyar Lake, where boat safaris provide glimpses of elephants, deer, and various bird species. The surrounding spice plantations and mist-clad hills enhance the scenic beauty.... With its focus on conservation and ecotourism, Periyar promises a captivating blend of adventure and tranquility in the heart of nature.

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

The Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Renowned for its intricate Dravidian architecture, the temple's towering gopuram and intricate sculptures captivate visitors. ... The deity, in a reclining posture on the sacred serpent Ananta, is a marvel of craftsmanship.Rich in history and spirituality, the temple also gained fame for its vaults housing unimaginable treasures. A visit to Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple offers a blend of cultural exploration and divine tranquility.

Eravikulam

Eravikulam National Park, situated in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, is a biodiversity hotspot and home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Known for its rolling hills, lush grasslands, and vibrant flora, the park offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes. The highlight is Anamudi, South India's highest peak, within the park boundaries. Visitors can explore the park through guided tours, spotting wildlife and enjoying ...the picturesque beauty of this protected area.Eravikulam National Park stands as a testament to Kerala's natural splendor.

Thekkadi

Thekkady, located in the Idukki district of Kerala, is renowned for its picturesque landscapes and the Periyar National Park. Nestled in the Western Ghats, Thekkady offers a unique wildlife experience with boat safaris on Periyar Lake, where elephants and other wildlife can be spotted. The spice-scented plantations add to the region's charm, providing an opportunity for spice tours. ...With its diverse flora and fauna, Thekkady is a captivating destination for nature lovers, offering a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility in the heart of Kerala.
